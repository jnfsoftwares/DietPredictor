Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how DietApp ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. DietApp is designed to work entirely on your device without requiring internet connectivity for its core functionality. All diet predictions and calculations are performed locally using a heuristic algorithm.

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any information, there is no data to use or share.

Data Storage

All data entered into the app (such as survey responses) remains stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit, store, or access this information on any external servers.

Third-Party Services

DietApp does not integrate with any third-party services or analytics platforms. The app is completely self-contained.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, this policy applies equally to all users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated policy available within the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the GitHub repository: https://github.com/jnfsoftwares/DietApp
